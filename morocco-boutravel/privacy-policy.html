Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Morocco BouTravel collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when visitors contact us, request a tour, make a booking enquiry, or communicate with us through the website, WhatsApp, phone, email, or social media.

Information we collect

When you contact Morocco BouTravel or send a booking enquiry, we may collect the information you choose to provide, including your full name, email address, phone or WhatsApp number, preferred travel date, number of travelers, selected tour, pickup location, hotel or riad details, destination preferences, dietary requests, special requests, and message content.

For multi-day tours, we may also ask for information needed to organize the service, such as accommodation preferences, room arrangement, arrival details, flight or train information, luggage needs, and any mobility or health information you voluntarily share so we can prepare the trip properly.

Technical information

Our website may collect limited technical information needed for normal operation, performance, security, and user experience. This may include browser type, device type, pages visited, approximate location, referral source, and interaction data if analytics or similar tools are active.

How we use your information

We use personal information to reply to enquiries, check availability, prepare tour quotes, confirm itineraries, arrange pickup and transfers, coordinate guides, drivers, accommodation, meals, activities, and special requests, communicate before and during the trip, improve our services, prevent misuse, and keep necessary business records.

Communication channels

Morocco BouTravel may communicate with guests through WhatsApp, phone, email, social media, or external travel platforms. When you choose to use these channels, your information may also be processed according to the privacy policies of those external services.

Legal basis

We process personal information because it is necessary to respond to enquiries, prepare quotes, take steps before confirming a booking, provide requested travel services, manage customer communication, meet legal or accounting obligations, protect legitimate business interests, and, where required, based on consent.

Sharing personal information

We do not sell personal information. We may share only the necessary booking details with trusted service providers when required to deliver the requested service, such as drivers, local guides, hotels, desert camps, riads, restaurants, activity providers, transport partners, payment providers, or booking platforms. We share only the information needed for the service.

Payments

If payments or deposits are arranged, payment information may be processed by banks, payment providers, booking platforms, or other secure payment services. Morocco BouTravel should not request or store full card details through unsecured channels such as normal messages or email.

Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary to respond to enquiries, manage bookings, provide travel services, handle disputes, comply with legal or accounting requirements, and improve customer service. When information is no longer needed, it should be deleted, anonymized, or securely archived.

Cookies and external tools

The website may use essential technical features and may include links or embedded tools from external services such as Google Maps, WhatsApp, Instagram, social media platforms, analytics tools, or booking and payment services. These services may use their own cookies or tracking technologies. Visitors can manage cookies through their browser settings.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. However, no website, email, messaging app, or online transmission can be guaranteed completely secure. Visitors should avoid sending sensitive identity or payment information through unsecured channels.

International visitors

Morocco BouTravel may receive enquiries from visitors outside Morocco. By contacting us or requesting a booking, visitors understand that their information may be processed in Morocco and through international communication platforms or service providers used to organize the travel service.

Your rights

Depending on applicable law, visitors may request access to their personal information, ask for correction, request deletion, object to certain processing, or withdraw consent where applicable. To make a request, visitors can contact us through the details listed on the Contact page.

Children and family trips

Our tours may be suitable for families, but booking requests should be made by adults. If information about children is provided as part of a family booking, it will only be used to organize the requested travel service.

External links

Our website may link to external websites and apps such as Google Maps, WhatsApp, Instagram, social media platforms, payment providers, accommodation providers, or booking platforms. Morocco BouTravel is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of these third-party services.

Updates to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, website, legal requirements, or business practices. The latest version will be published on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions or personal data requests, please contact Morocco BouTravel through the contact details listed on the Contact page.
