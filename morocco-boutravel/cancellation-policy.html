Cancellation Policy

This Cancellation Policy explains how cancellations, refunds, date changes, no-shows, weather issues, and supplier changes are handled for tours and travel experiences booked with Morocco BouTravel. By confirming a booking, guests agree to the cancellation terms below.

How to cancel or change a booking

To cancel or change a booking, guests must contact Morocco BouTravel as early as possible by WhatsApp or email. The request should include the guest name, booking date, selected tour, number of travelers, and confirmation details. Cancellation and change requests are considered received only when our team confirms receipt.

Day trips and local excursions

For standard day trips and local excursions, including Atlas Mountains day trips, Essaouira day trips, Ouzoud waterfalls day trips, Marrakech activities, and similar one-day programs, the following rules apply unless different terms were agreed in writing:

  • Cancellations made 48 hours or more before the scheduled departure time are eligible for a full refund.
  • Cancellations made between 24 and 48 hours before the scheduled departure time are eligible for a 50% refund.
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled departure time are non-refundable.
  • No-shows are non-refundable.

Sahara desert tours and multi-day trips

For Sahara desert tours, imperial cities tours, Chefchaouen and North Morocco tours, coastal tours, and other multi-day itineraries, the following rules apply unless different terms were agreed in writing:

  • Cancellations made 7 days or more before departure are eligible for a full refund, excluding non-refundable supplier costs already paid.
  • Cancellations made between 3 and 7 days before departure are eligible for a 50% refund.
  • Cancellations made less than 72 hours before departure are non-refundable.
  • No-shows are non-refundable.

Private tours and custom itineraries

Private tours, custom programs, photography trips, group tours, special accommodation requests, and tailor-made itineraries require advance planning and supplier coordination. For these bookings, cancellations made 14 days or more before departure are eligible for a full refund excluding non-refundable supplier costs already paid. Cancellations made between 7 and 14 days before departure are eligible for a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 7 days before departure are non-refundable.

Deposits

Some bookings may require a deposit to secure drivers, guides, accommodation, desert camps, activities, meals, or special arrangements. Deposit refundability follows the cancellation rules for the booked program. If suppliers or special services have already been reserved or paid for, those non-refundable costs may be deducted from any eligible refund.

No-shows and late arrivals

Guests who do not arrive at the agreed pickup point or departure location without contacting us may be considered a no-show. No-shows are non-refundable. Late arrivals may reduce the duration of the tour and may not qualify for a refund or extension, especially when drivers, guides, accommodation, activities, or long-distance routes are already scheduled.

Pickup changes

Pickup arrangements depend on location, road access, driver availability, traffic, and timing. If guests change the pickup location or pickup time after confirmation, additional fees may apply. If the driver has already started the journey, transfer or transport fees may be non-refundable.

Accommodation and supplier costs

For multi-day tours, some hotels, riads, desert camps, restaurants, guides, transport providers, and activity suppliers may apply their own cancellation rules. Non-refundable supplier costs already paid on behalf of the guest may be deducted from any eligible refund.

Weather, safety, and operational changes

Tours in Morocco may be affected by weather, wind, rain, snow in mountain areas, heat, road conditions, traffic, attraction closures, or safety concerns. If Morocco BouTravel must adjust a tour for weather, safety, or operational reasons, we may offer a route change, time change, date change, alternative activity, travel credit, or refund depending on the situation.

Date changes and rescheduling

Date changes may be possible depending on availability, season, accommodation, drivers, guides, and supplier conditions. Please request changes as early as possible. Price differences may apply if the new date, traveler number, accommodation level, or itinerary details change.

Refund processing

Approved refunds are processed using the original payment method where possible. Processing times may vary depending on the bank, payment provider, or booking platform. Transfer fees, bank fees, platform fees, and currency conversion differences may not be refundable.

External booking platforms

If a booking is made through an external platform or travel marketplace, the cancellation and refund terms shown on that platform may also apply. Guests should check their booking confirmation and contact the platform when required.

Contact

For cancellation, refund, or date-change requests, please contact Morocco BouTravel through the WhatsApp, phone, or email details listed on the Contact page.
